Traditional Coffee

Coffee is a popular drink that is loved and chosen the most today. Black coffee, milk coffee made from pure coffee beans on the land of Robusta, Arabica or weasel coffee, etc., are considered traditional coffee with strong flavor. Depending on the needs and preferences, diners can choose 1 cup of coffee made directly or ready to drink hot or with ice. Those who love pure coffee often choose 1 cup of black coffee without sugar, or can add 1-2 teaspoons of sugar. And those who like sweet, fragrant, will ask for a cup of rich, sweet milk coffee.
