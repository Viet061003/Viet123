    CafeLatte

    Cafe Latte simply means coffee and milk. For those who are not connoisseurs, it is easy to confuse Cafe Latte with Cappuchino because these two types share the same 3 main ingredients. However, the difference is that, when making Cafe Latte, people only give half the amount of milk foam as hot milk; instead of equalizing like in cappuccino. An Espresso is prepared with milk and milk powder in a 200ml glass or cup with a temperature of 600 and then 0.5 - 1cm of milk powder on the surface.
