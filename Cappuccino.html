    Cappuccino

    Also originating from Italy, a standard Cappuccino will consist of 3 equal parts, that is: Espresso coffee mixed with double the amount of water (Espresso Lungo), hot milk and foamed milk. Next, sprinkle a little more cocoa powder or cinnamon powder on top, then use a mold, spoon or toothpick to stir/draw to create shapes for your products, be it a heart shape, a leaf shape, a cloud, etc. equally eye-catching.
